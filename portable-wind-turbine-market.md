<p><ul><li>Horizontal-Axis Wind Turbine</li><li>Vertical-Axis Wind Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable wind turbines come in two main types: horizontal-axis and vertical-axis. Horizontal-axis wind turbines are the more common type and have blades that rotate around a horizontal axis. They are efficient in moderate to high wind speeds. On the other hand, vertical-axis wind turbines have blades that rotate around a vertical axis, making them suitable for low wind speeds and turbulent conditions. Both types of portable wind turbines are compact and easy to transport, making them ideal for off-grid applications.</p></p>
