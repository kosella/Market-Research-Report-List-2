<p><p>Synthetic Textile Flooring comes in various types, including Tufting, Woven, and Needlefelt. Tufting involves creating a pile surface by injecting yarns into a backing material. Woven flooring is made by interlacing yarns in a pattern, creating a durable and versatile option. Needlefelt flooring is produced by punching fibers through a backing material, resulting in a dense and durable product. Each type offers unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different preferences and requirements in the market.</p></p>
