Pneumatic tires are filled with air and provide a cushioned ride, suitable for outdoor use. Solid tires are made of solid rubber and are puncture-proof, ideal for heavy-duty indoor applications. Each type caters to different needs and applications within the industrial truck industry.</p></p>
