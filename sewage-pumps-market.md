<p><p>Sewage pumps are used to efficiently move wastewater from one location to another. There are two main types of sewage pumps: Day sewage pumps and Submersible sewage pumps. Day sewage pumps are typically installed outside the water source and require a dry installation area. Submersible sewage pumps are designed to be submerged in the liquid being pumped, providing a more compact and quieter operation. Both types of sewage pumps play a crucial role in managing wastewater in various applications.</p></p>
