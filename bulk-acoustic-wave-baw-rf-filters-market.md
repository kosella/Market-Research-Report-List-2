<p><ul><li>FBAR</li><li>SMR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) RF Filters have two main types: Film Bulk Acoustic Resonator (FBAR) and Solidly Mounted Resonator (SMR). FBAR filters use a thin film of piezoelectric material to generate acoustic waves, while SMR filters are built directly on the acoustic stack. FBAR filters are preferred for their high performance in mobile devices, while SMR filters are commonly used in infrastructure applications. Both types serve different market needs in the telecommunications industry, offering diverse solutions for RF filtering requirements.</p></p>
