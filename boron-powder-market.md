<p><strong>The Boron Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Amorphous</li><li>Crystalline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boron powder is available in two main types: amorphous and crystalline. </p><p>Amorphous boron powder has a disordered atomic structure, giving it unique properties such as high chemical reactivity and good thermal resistance. It is used in industries like aerospace and electronics for its versatility.</p><p>Crystalline boron powder has a regular atomic structure, making it harder and more stable compared to amorphous boron. It is commonly used in industries like metallurgy and nuclear energy due to its high strength and conductivity.</p></p>
