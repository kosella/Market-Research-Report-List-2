<p><p>SMD Potentiometers are electronic components used to adjust resistance in circuits. They are available in two main types: Digital and Analog. Digital potentiometers provide precise digital control over resistance values, making them ideal for applications requiring accurate adjustments. Analog potentiometers, on the other hand, offer smooth and continuous adjustment of resistance, making them suitable for applications where fine-tuning is needed. Both types cater to different market needs, with digital potentiometers being preferred for digital applications and analog potentiometers for analog applications.</p></p>
