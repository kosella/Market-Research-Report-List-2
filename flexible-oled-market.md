<p><p>Flexible OLED displays are categorized into two main market types: Crystalline and Amorphous. Crystalline OLED technology involves the use of organic light-emitting diodes arranged in a crystalline structure, providing improved efficiency and color accuracy. On the other hand, Amorphous OLED technology uses randomly arranged OLEDs, offering flexibility and durability. Crystalline OLED displays are known for their high performance and image quality, while Amorphous OLED displays are valued for their flexibility and bendable capabilities, making them suitable for various applications in the market.</p></p>
