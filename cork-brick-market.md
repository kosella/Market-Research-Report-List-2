<p><p>Cork bricks come in two main market types: round hole and non-round hole. Round hole cork bricks have a uniform design with evenly spaced holes throughout the surface, allowing for easy insertion of other materials. Non-round hole cork bricks, on the other hand, have irregularly shaped holes or no holes at all, providing a more solid structure for construction projects. Both market types offer unique benefits and can be used for various applications in building and design projects.</p></p>
