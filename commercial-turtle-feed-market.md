<p><p>The commercial turtle feed market consists of two main types: staple and supplement. Staple turtle feeds are nutritionally balanced diets that provide all the essential nutrients needed for normal growth and development. These feeds are typically the main source of nutrition for turtles and are designed to be fed on a daily basis. Supplement turtle feeds, on the other hand, are additional food products that are meant to be fed in addition to staple feeds.
