<p><p>Dynamic tunable module market consists of three main types: Wavelength Selective Switch (WSS), Micro-Optical Cross Connect (OXC), and Multiservice Transport Platform (MSTP). WSS modules allow for flexible wavelength routing and switching in optical networks. OXC modules provide dynamic cross-connect functionality for efficient traffic management. MSTPs offer a range of services including wavelength grooming and aggregation. These modules are essential in enabling dynamic reconfiguration of optical networks to adapt to varying traffic demands and optimize network efficiency.</p></p>
