<p><p>Straw blankets are erosion control products used to protect soil, prevent erosion, and promote vegetation growth. Single Net Straw Blankets have one layer of biodegradable netting on top of the straw, providing basic erosion control. Double Net Straw Blankets have an additional layer of netting on the bottom, offering increased stability and strength for more challenging terrains. Both types are effective in controlling erosion and promoting vegetation growth, but Double Net Straw Blankets are better suited for areas with higher erosion risk or slope.</p></p>
