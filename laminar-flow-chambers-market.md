<p><strong>The Laminar Flow Chambers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminar flow chambers are typically classified into two types based on their orientation: vertical and horizontal. Vertical laminar flow chambers are designed for applications where maximum protection against contamination is required, such as in drug compounding and cell culture work. Horizontal laminar flow chambers, on the other hand, are more commonly used for applications where user ergonomics and accessibility are key, such as in laboratory workspaces with limited vertical space. Both types offer the benefits of providing a controlled sterile environment for sensitive procedures.</p></p>
