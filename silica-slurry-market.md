<p><p>Silica slurry market is categorized into two types, micron grade and nano grade. Micron grade silica slurry consists of particles that are larger in size, typically in the range of one to ten microns. On the other hand, nano grade silica slurry contains particles that are extremely small, typically less than 100 nanometers in size. These different grades cater to specific applications where either larger or smaller particle sizes are required for optimal performance.</p></p>
