<p><strong>The Photomask Review System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photomask Review System market includes two main types: Die to Die (DD) Method and Die to Database (DB) Method. In the DD Method, the system compares two individual masks, known as a reference and a test mask, to identify any differences or defects between them. On the other hand, the DB Method compares the mask layout data directly with the simulated mask image data to ensure accuracy and consistency. Both methods play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of photomasks used in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
