<p><p>Gourmet powder is a common seasoning used in Asian cuisine. It is available in two main market types: powder and granule. Gourmet powder in powder form is finely ground and easily dissolves in liquids, making it ideal for soups, sauces, and marinades. On the other hand, gourmet powder in granule form is coarser and adds a nice texture to dishes such as stir-fries and fried rice. Both types of gourmet powder enhance the flavor of dishes and are a staple in many Asian kitchens.</p></p>
