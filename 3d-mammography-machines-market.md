<p><p>Computed radiography machines, flat panel machines, and CMOS flat machines are three types of 3D mammography machines commonly used in the medical field. Computed radiography machines use a cassette-based system, while flat panel machines utilize a digital detector. CMOS flat machines use a complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor sensor to capture images. Each type has its own unique features and benefits, but all are designed to provide high-quality, detailed images for the early detection and diagnosis of breast cancer.</p></p>
