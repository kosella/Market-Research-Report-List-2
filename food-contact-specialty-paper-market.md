<p><p>Food contact specialty paper market includes various types such as greaseproof paper, waxed paper, foil paper, and others. Greaseproof paper is resistant to oil and grease, making it ideal for packaging oily foods. Waxed paper has a coating of wax on one or both sides, providing a barrier against moisture. Foil paper is made of aluminum, offering a barrier against light, oxygen, and moisture. Other specialty papers may include parchment paper, kraft paper, and coated paper, each serving a specific purpose in food packaging.</p></p>
