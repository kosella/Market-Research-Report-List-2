<p><p>PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is a versatile material used in gaskets for various applications. The market is segmented into three types: Virgin PTFE, which is pure and offers excellent chemical resistance; Filled PTFE, which is reinforced with materials like glass fibers or carbon to enhance strength and wear resistance; and Expanded PTFE, which is softer and more flexible than other types, ideal for sealing irregular surfaces. Each type has unique properties that cater to specific needs in industries such as chemical processing, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
