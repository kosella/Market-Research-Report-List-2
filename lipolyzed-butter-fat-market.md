<p><p>Lipolyzed Butter Fat comes in two main types: Raw Butter and Ultrafine Butter. Raw Butter is made by churning cream and separating the milk fats, while Ultrafine Butter is produced by breaking down the raw butter into smaller particles through a process called lipolysis. Raw Butter retains more of its natural flavor and texture, while Ultrafine Butter is smoother and more easily incorporated into various products. Both types are commonly used in the food industry for adding richness and flavor to a wide range of dishes.</p></p>
