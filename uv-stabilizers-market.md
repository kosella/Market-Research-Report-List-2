<p><p>UV stabilizers are additives used in various industries to protect materials from degradation caused by exposure to UV radiation. There are three main types of UV stabilizers: Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS) which work by scavenging free radicals generated by UV light, UV absorbers which absorb UV radiation and convert it into heat, and quenchers which interrupt the chain reaction of photo-oxidation. Each type of UV stabilizer offers unique benefits and is used in different applications depending on the specific requirements of the material being protected.</p></p>
