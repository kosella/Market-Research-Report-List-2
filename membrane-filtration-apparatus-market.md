<p><p>Membrane filtration apparatus is used in various industries for separation processes. The market is divided into four main types based on pore size and efficiency: Microfiltration, Ultrafiltration, Nanofiltration, and Reverse Osmosis. Microfiltration is used for the removal of large particles, while Ultrafiltration is effective in separating macromolecules. Nanofiltration is used for smaller molecules and ions, and Reverse Osmosis is the most advanced type, capable of removing even smaller particles and ions. These technologies cater to different needs in industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing.</p></p>
