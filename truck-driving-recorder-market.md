<p><p>Truck Driving Recorder market consists of two main types: Integrated and Portable. Integrated recorders are built directly into the truck's dashboard, providing a permanent and seamless recording solution. They offer advanced features and are often preferred by professional truck drivers. On the other hand, Portable recorders are smaller devices that can be easily moved between vehicles. They are popular among occasional drivers and individuals looking for a more affordable and flexible recording option. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
