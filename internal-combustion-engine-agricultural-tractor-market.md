<p><p>Internal Combustion Engine Agricultural Tractors are commonly available in two types - 2WD and 4WD. 2WD tractors are designed to provide power and traction to only two wheels, typically the rear wheels. They are usually more affordable and suitable for flat, even terrains. On the other hand, 4WD tractors provide power to all four wheels, offering better traction and performance on rough, uneven terrains. They are more expensive but are preferred for heavy-duty tasks and operations in challenging agricultural conditions.</p></p>
