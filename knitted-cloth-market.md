<p><strong>The Knitted Cloth Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Warp Knitting</li><li>Weft Knitting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Knitted cloth market is divided into two main types - warp knitting and weft knitting. In warp knitting, each loop in the fabric is formed along the length of the fabric, resulting in a more stable and durable material. Weft knitting, on the other hand, involves loops being formed across the width of the fabric, giving it more elasticity and flexibility. Both types of knitting have their own advantages and are used in various industries such as apparel, home textiles, and technical textiles.</p></p>
