<p><strong>The Lab Vacuum Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Delivery Pump</li><li>Gas Adsorption Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lab vacuum pumps are essential in laboratories for creating a vacuum environment. Gas delivery pumps are used for delivering gases into the system, while gas adsorption pumps remove gases from the system by adsorbing them onto a surface. Gas delivery pumps are commonly used in gas chromatography and mass spectrometry applications, while gas adsorption pumps are used in semiconductor manufacturing and vacuum furnaces. These pumps play a crucial role in maintaining a clean and controlled environment in laboratory settings.</p></p>
