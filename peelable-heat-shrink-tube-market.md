<p><p>Peelable heat shrink tubes are available in two main market types: clear and unclear. Clear peelable heat shrink tubes are transparent, allowing for easy visibility of the underlying components after installation. This makes them ideal for applications where visual inspection is necessary. On the other hand, unclear peelable heat shrink tubes have an opaque appearance, providing added protection against UV exposure and other environmental factors. Both types offer reliable insulation and moisture resistance, catering to different preferences and requirements in the market.</p></p>
