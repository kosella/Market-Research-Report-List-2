<p><strong>The OCTG Connectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>API Connections</li><li>Semi-premium & Premium Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OCTG connectors market can be divided into three main types based on the level of performance and quality: API connections, semi-premium connections, and premium connections. API connections are standard connections that meet the specifications set by the American Petroleum Institute. Semi-premium connections offer improved sealing and torque capabilities compared to API connections. Premium connections are the highest quality, offering the best sealing, torque resistance, and fatigue performance. As a result, premium connections are typically used in demanding oil and gas drilling operations.</p></p>
