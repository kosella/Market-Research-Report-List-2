<p><p>Industrial ozone generators can be classified into two main types based on their cooling mechanisms: air cooled and water cooled. Air cooled ozone generators use fans to dissipate heat generated during the ozone generation process, making them suitable for applications where water availability or quality is a concern. On the other hand, water cooled ozone generators use water as a coolant to remove heat, making them more efficient and effective in certain industrial settings. Both types offer unique benefits and are used in various industries for air and water purification purposes.</p></p>
