<p><p>Electrophoresis Instrumentation Market includes three main types: Blotting Instruments, Vertical Gel Electrophoresis, and Horizontal Gel Electrophoresis. Blotting instruments are used for transferring molecules from a gel to a membrane. Vertical gel electrophoresis separates molecules based on size in a vertical orientation. Horizontal gel electrophoresis separates molecules based on size in a horizontal orientation. Each type of instrumentation plays a crucial role in various scientific applications such as DNA analysis, protein separation, and drug discovery in research laboratories and clinical settings.</p></p>
