<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ultraviolet Stabilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hindered Amine Light Stabilizer (HALS)</li><li>UV Absorbers</li><li>Quenchers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultraviolet stabilizers are used to prevent degradation of materials exposed to UV radiation. Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS) act by scavenging free radicals generated by UV light. UV Absorbers work by absorbing and dissipating UV energy, protecting the material underneath. Quenchers function by deactivating excited states of molecules produced by UV radiation, preventing them from causing damage. These different types of stabilizers cater to various applications and provide protection against UV-induced degradation in a wide range of industries.</p></p>
