<p><p>Tidal stream generators are a type of renewable energy technology that harnesses the power of ocean currents to generate electricity. There are different types of tidal stream generators available in the market, including axial turbines, crossflow turbines, flow augmented turbines, and others. Axial turbines have blades that rotate around a central axis, while crossflow turbines have blades that rotate perpendicular to the flow of water. Flow augmented turbines utilize additional structures to increase the velocity of the water flowing through the turbine, resulting in higher power output.</p></p>
