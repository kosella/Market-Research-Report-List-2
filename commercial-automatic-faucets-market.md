<p><p>Commercial automatic faucets can be categorized into two types based on power source: DC Power and AC Power. On the other hand, AC Power faucets are powered by alternating current electricity, providing a reliable and consistent power supply. Both types offer hands-free operation, helping to promote hygiene and reduce water waste in commercial settings.</p></p>
