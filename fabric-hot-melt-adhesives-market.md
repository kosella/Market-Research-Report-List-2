<p><strong>The Fabric Hot-Melt Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives</li><li>Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives do not undergo any chemical reaction during the bonding process. They rely on physical mechanisms, such as cooling and setting, to achieve adhesion. These adhesives are often used in applications where fast bonding speed and easy processing are required. </p><p>Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives, on the other hand, contain ingredients that chemically react with each other to form a durable bond. These adhesives offer higher bond strength and heat resistance compared to non-reactive adhesives, making them suitable for demanding applications such as automotive assembly and construction.</p></p>
