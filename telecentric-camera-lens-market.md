<p><strong>The Telecentric Camera Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Object Square Telephoto Lens</li><li>Bi-Telecentric Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Telecentric camera lenses are used in machine vision systems for various applications. The object square telephoto lens is a type of telecentric lens that provides a high level of image quality and consistency by ensuring that the object being imaged is square to the lens axis. On the other hand, bi-telecentric lenses are designed to provide telecentricity on both the object and image sides, ensuring accurate and distortion-free imaging. Both types of lenses are crucial in industries like manufacturing, robotics, and inspection for precise measurements and analysis.</p></p>
