<p><p>Microwave digesters are used in laboratories for sample preparation in analytical chemistry. They are available in two main types: desktop and floor-standing. Desktop digesters are smaller in size and can be placed on a laboratory bench for convenient access and use, making them suitable for smaller labs with limited space. Floor-standing digesters, on the other hand, are larger and more powerful, suitable for high-throughput labs that process a larger number of samples and require higher capacity and efficiency.</p></p>
