<p><strong>The Planar Diffused Photodiodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PN Type</li><li>PIN Type</li><li>Avalanche Type</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Planar diffused photodiodes are categorized into four main types based on their structure and operating principles. PN type photodiodes have a PN junction formed within the semiconductor material. PIN type photodiodes have an additional intrinsic layer between the P and N regions, allowing for improved sensitivity. Avalanche type photodiodes operate based on the avalanche effect, providing higher gain and sensitivity. Other types in the market include schottky photodiodes and phototransistors, each offering unique features and applications.</p></p>
