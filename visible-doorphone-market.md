<p><strong>The Visible Doorphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wired</li><li>Wireless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Visible doorphones are prevalent in both wired and wireless markets. Wired doorphones require a physical connection between the doorphone and the indoor monitor or receiver. This type ensures a stable and reliable connection, ideal for buildings with a structured wiring system in place. On the other hand, wireless doorphones operate through radio frequency signals, providing more flexibility in installation and allowing for easy relocation. This type is suitable for locations where running wires is difficult or not feasible. Both markets cater to different needs and preferences of consumers.</p></p>
