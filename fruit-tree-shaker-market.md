<p><p>Mounted fruit tree shakers are attached to tractors or other vehicles for operation. They are ideal for orchards with rows of trees where the shaker can easily move between each row. Self-propelled shakers are standalone machines that do not require a separate vehicle for operation. They offer more flexibility in terms of maneuverability and can access hard-to-reach areas. Trailed shakers are towed behind a vehicle and are suitable for larger orchards where efficiency and productivity are important.</p></p>
