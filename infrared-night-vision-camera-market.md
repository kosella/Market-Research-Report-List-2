<p><strong>The Infrared Night Vision Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Imaging</li><li>Near Infrared Imaging</li><li>Low Light Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared night vision cameras come in three main types: thermal imaging, near-infrared imaging, and low light imaging. Thermal imaging cameras detect heat signatures to create a visible image, making them ideal for detecting objects in complete darkness. Near-infrared imaging cameras use near-infrared light to capture images in low light conditions. Low light imaging cameras amplify available light to produce clear images in dark environments. Each type of camera has specific applications in security, surveillance, and night-time photography.</p></p>
