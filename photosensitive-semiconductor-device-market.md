<p><ul><li>Photocell</li><li>Photodiode</li><li>Phototransistor</li><li>Photoresistor</li><li>Photo IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photosensitive semiconductor devices are electronic components that convert light into an electrical signal. The market includes various types such as photocells, photodiodes, phototransistors, photoresistors, and photo ICs. Photocells are light-sensitive resistors that change resistance in response to light intensity. Photodiodes are diodes that convert light into current. Phototransistors amplify light signals. Photoresistors change resistance based on light. Photo ICs integrate light-sensing elements with signal processing circuitry. Each type serves different applications in industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and healthcare.</p></p>
