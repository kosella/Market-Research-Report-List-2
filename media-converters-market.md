<p><p>Media converters are devices used to convert signals between different types of media, such as copper and fiber optic cables. Copper-to-Fiber media converters are used to convert Ethernet signals from copper cables to fiber optic cables, allowing for longer transmission distances and increased bandwidth. Fiber-to-Fiber media converters are used to extend fiber optic networks by converting signals between different types of fiber cables, such as multimode to single mode. Both types of media converters are essential for integrating different network technologies and extending network reach.</p></p>
