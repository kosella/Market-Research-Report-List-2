<p><p>Latex Rubber Thread market types refer to the different coatings applied to rubber threads for various purposes. Talc Coated Rubber Thread (TCR) is coated with talc powder to improve elasticity and reduce friction. Silicon Coated Rubber Thread (SCR) is coated with silicone for increased durability and water resistance. These coatings enhance the performance and longevity of rubber threads, making them suitable for different applications such as textile, apparel, and medical industries. Each type caters to specific needs and requirements in the market.</p></p>
