<p><p>Arc flash protection relays are crucial in protecting both equipment and personnel from the dangers of arc flash incidents. In the medium voltage (MV) market, these relays are designed to detect arc flash events in electrical systems operating at higher voltages, typically between 1kV and 38kV. In the low voltage (LV) market, arc flash relays are tailored for systems operating at lower voltages, usually below 1kV. Both MV and LV arc flash protection relays work by rapidly detecting and isolating any potential arc flash events to minimize damage and ensure safety.</p></p>
