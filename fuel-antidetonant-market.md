<p><strong>The Fuel Antidetonant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MMT</li><li>MTBE</li><li>TAME</li><li>TBA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuel antidetonant market types include MMT, MTBE, TAME, and TBA, each serving the purpose of preventing engine knocking in vehicles. MMT is a manganese-based additive, MTBE is an oxygenate derived from methanol, TAME is an ether-based substance, and TBA is a tertiary butyl alcohol compound. These additives are used in gasoline to improve engine performance by increasing its octane rating and reducing harmful emissions. Each type has its own unique characteristics and benefits in terms of fuel efficiency and engine protection.</p></p>
