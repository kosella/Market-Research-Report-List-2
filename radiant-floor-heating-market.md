<p><strong>The Radiant Floor Heating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Hydronic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiant floor heating systems are available in two main types: electric and hydronic. Electric radiant floor heating utilizes electric cables or mats installed beneath the floor to generate heat. This type of system is typically easier and less expensive to install, making it a popular choice for smaller spaces. On the other hand, hydronic radiant floor heating uses hot water pipes to heat the floor. While hydronic systems are more complex and costly to install, they are generally more efficient and can be used to heat larger areas.</p></p>
