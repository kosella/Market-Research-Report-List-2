<p><p>Specialty Carbon Black for Polymers market includes various types such as Lamp Black, Acetylene Black, Gas Black, and Others. Lamp Black is produced by incomplete combustion of oil or gas and is primarily used in rubber applications. Acetylene Black is made from the decomposition of acetylene gas and is known for its high structure and conductivity, making it suitable for battery electrodes. Gas Black is derived from natural gas and is used in various industrial applications. Other specialty carbon blacks include channel blacks, thermal blacks, and furnace blacks with unique properties for different polymer applications.</p></p>
