<p><strong>The Food Mercury Analysis Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Type</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food Mercury Analysis Systems utilize various technologies to detect and measure mercury levels in food samples. Two common types of systems include Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Type and Cold Atomic Fluorescence Type. Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Type systems use a vaporization step followed by absorption of mercury atoms, while Cold Atomic Fluorescence Type systems detect mercury levels through fluorescence emission. Both types are employed in food industry for ensuring food safety and compliance with regulations regarding mercury contamination.</p></p>
