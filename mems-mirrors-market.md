<p><strong>The MEMS Mirrors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1D</li><li>2D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS mirrors are categorized into two main types based on the number of axes they can move along: 1D and 2D mirrors. </p><p>1D MEMS mirrors can move along one axis, typically tilting up and down or left and right. They are commonly used in applications such as laser scanning, optical switches, and thermal imaging.</p><p>2D MEMS mirrors, on the other hand, can move along two axes, allowing for more complex and precise control of the mirror's position. They find applications in fields such as virtual reality, automotive LiDAR, and laser projection.</p></p>
