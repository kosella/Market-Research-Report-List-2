<p><p>Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy (CRRT) is a type of blood purification therapy used for patients with acute kidney injury or chronic kidney disease. It involves slow and continuous removal of waste products and excess fluids from the blood, mimicking the normal function of the kidneys. CRRT is typically used in critically ill patients in intensive care units.</p><p>A Hemodialysis Machine, on the other hand, is a device used for patients with end-stage renal disease to remove waste and excess fluids from the blood. It works by circulating the blood through a dialyzer, a machine that acts as an artificial kidney, removing toxins and balancing electrolytes in the blood.</p><p>The CRRT and Hemodialysis Machine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 3.20% during the forecast period. The market growth is being driven by the increasing prevalence of kidney diseases, rising geriatric population, and technological advancements in the healthcare industry. Additionally, the growing awareness about kidney health and the increasing number of dialysis centers worldwide are contributing to the market growth. The latest trends in the market include the development of portable and user-friendly dialysis machines, increasing adoption of home-based dialysis treatments, and the integration of artificial intelligence and telemedicine in dialysis care.</p></p>
