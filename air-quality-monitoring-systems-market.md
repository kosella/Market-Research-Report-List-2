<p><p>Air Quality Monitoring Systems market includes Portable Monitoring Systems and Stationary Monitoring Systems. Portable Monitoring Systems are compact, lightweight devices that can be easily carried to various locations for real-time air quality monitoring. These are commonly used for personal exposure monitoring or in areas where stationary systems are not feasible. On the other hand, Stationary Monitoring Systems are fixed in specific locations and continuously monitor air quality in that area. These systems are commonly used in urban areas, industrial sites, or near sources of pollution.</p></p>
