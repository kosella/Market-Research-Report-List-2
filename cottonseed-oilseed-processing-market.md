<p><p>Cottonseed oilseed processing can be divided into two main types: mechanical processing and chemical processing. </p><p>Mechanical processing involves physically extracting oil from the cottonseeds using mechanical methods such as pressing or expelling. This method is generally considered more natural and retains more nutrients in the oil.</p><p>Chemical processing, on the other hand, involves using chemical solvents to extract oil from the seeds. This method is more efficient and yields a higher quantity of oil, but some argue that it may result in a lower quality product.</p></p>
