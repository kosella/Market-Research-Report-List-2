<p><strong>The Polyimide Tubing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seamless Tubing</li><li>Spiral Wound Tubing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyimide tubing market includes two main types, seamless tubing and spiral wound tubing. Seamless tubing is manufactured from a single piece of material, providing a smooth and continuous surface that is ideal for high-pressure applications. Spiral wound tubing, on the other hand, is constructed by wrapping a flat strip of polyimide material into a helical shape, offering flexibility and resistance to kinking. Both types of tubing are commonly used in industries such as aerospace, electronics, and automotive for their excellent thermal and chemical properties.</p></p>
