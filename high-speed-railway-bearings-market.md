<p><p>High-speed railway bearings market is divided into two main types: ball bearings and sliding bearings. Ball bearings are commonly used in high-speed applications due to their ability to withstand high speeds and heavy loads. They are designed to reduce friction and support radial and axial loads. On the other hand, sliding bearings are used for slower moving parts that require less maintenance. They rely on a sliding motion between two surfaces to reduce friction and support loads in high-speed railway applications.</p></p>
