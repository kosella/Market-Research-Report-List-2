<p><p>The Submarine Power Cables market consists of two major types: Single Core Cable and Multi Core Cable. Single Core Cables are designed with a single conductor to transmit electricity, while Multi Core Cables have multiple conductors bundled together within the same cable. Single Core Cables are commonly used for long distance power transmission, while Multi Core Cables are preferred for applications where multiple power sources or connections are required. Both types play a critical role in providing reliable power transmission across underwater environments.</p></p>
