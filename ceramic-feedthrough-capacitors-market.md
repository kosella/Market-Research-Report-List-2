<p><p>Ceramic feedthrough capacitors can be categorized into two main types: plug-in and patch capacitors. Plug-in capacitors are directly inserted into a circuit board, providing a convenient and secure connection. Patch capacitors, on the other hand, are surface-mounted onto the circuit board, often used in compact electronic devices where space is limited. Both types of capacitors offer high reliability and performance in filtering out unwanted noise and interference in electronic circuits.</p></p>
