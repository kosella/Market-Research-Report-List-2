<p><p>Nano Crystalline Cellulose (NCC) is produced through two main methods: Sulfuric Acid Hydrolysis and Enzymatic Hydrolysis. The sulfuric acid method involves sulfuric acid breaking down cellulose into nano-sized crystals, while the enzymatic method involves enzymes breaking down cellulose. Each method has its own advantages and applications. The sulfuric acid method is more common and cost-effective, while the enzymatic method is more environmentally friendly and produces higher quality NCC. Both methods cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
