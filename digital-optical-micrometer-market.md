<p><p>Digital optical micrometers are available in two main market types: Single-axis and Multi-axis. Single-axis micrometers measure dimensions in one direction, providing high accuracy for linear measurements. Multi-axis micrometers, on the other hand, can measure dimensions in multiple directions simultaneously, offering versatility and efficiency for complex measurement tasks. Overall, single-axis micrometers are suitable for simpler applications with linear measurements, while multi-axis micrometers are ideal for more advanced and intricate measurement requirements.</p></p>
