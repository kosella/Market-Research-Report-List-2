<p><p>Polyethylene wax emulsion market types can be classified based on their properties as cationic, anionic, or non-ionic. Cationic emulsions have positively charged particles, making them suitable for various applications in industries like coatings and adhesives. Anionic emulsions contain negatively charged particles, providing stability and compatibility with other chemicals. Non-ionic emulsions have no charge and can be used in a wide range of products due to their versatility and compatibility with different substances. Each type offers unique benefits for different applications.</p></p>
