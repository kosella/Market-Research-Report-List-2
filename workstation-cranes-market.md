<p><p>Workstation cranes are essential material handling equipment used in various industrial settings. There are three main types of workstation cranes: floor-mounted, ceiling-mounted, and monorail. Floor-mounted workstation cranes are ideal for applications where the floor space is limited. Ceiling-mounted workstation cranes are suspended from the ceiling, allowing for maximum floor space utilization. Monorail workstation cranes consist of a single track that supports the lifting mechanism, making them suitable for linear material handling tasks. Each type caters to specific needs in different industries.</p></p>
