<p><strong>The Coolant Chillers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-Cooled Type</li><li>Water-Cooled Type</li><li>Evaporative Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coolant chillers are categorized into three main types based on the method of cooling – Air-Cooled Type, Water-Cooled Type, and Evaporative Type. Air-Cooled chillers use air to remove heat from the coolant, Water-Cooled chillers use water for cooling, and Evaporative Type chillers use evaporation to cool the coolant. Each type has its own advantages and applications, with Air-Cooled chillers being more energy efficient, Water-Cooled chillers offering consistent cooling performance, and Evaporative Type chillers being suitable for areas with limited water supply.</p></p>
