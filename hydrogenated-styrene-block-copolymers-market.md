<p><p>Hydrogenated Styrene Block Copolymers market types consist of Styrene Ethylene Propylene Styrene (SEPS) and Styrene Ethylene Butylene Styrene (SEBS). SEPS copolymers offer enhanced thermal stability and resilience, making them ideal for applications requiring high-temperature resistance. On the other hand, SEBS copolymers exhibit superior flexibility and elasticity, making them suitable for products that require high levels of impact resistance. Both copolymers have different properties that cater to specific applications in industries such as automotive, construction, and medical.</p></p>
