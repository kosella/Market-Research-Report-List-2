<p><p>Capacitive acceleration sensors are categorized based on the number of axes they measure movement on - 1 axis, 2 axis, and 3 axis. </p><p>1 axis sensors measure acceleration along a single direction, commonly used in applications requiring motion tracking in one specific direction. </p><p>2 axis sensors measure acceleration in two perpendicular directions, providing more comprehensive movement data compared to 1 axis sensors. </p><p>3 axis sensors measure movement in three dimensions, offering the most detailed and accurate data for applications requiring precise motion analysis in multiple directions.</p></p>
