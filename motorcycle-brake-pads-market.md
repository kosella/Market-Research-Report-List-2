<p><ul><li>Sintered Type</li><li>Ceramic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Motorcycle brake pads come in two main types: sintered and ceramic. Sintered brake pads are made from metal particles fused together at high temperatures, providing strong braking power and durability, making them ideal for high-performance or off-road motorcycles. On the other hand, ceramic brake pads are made from a mix of ceramic fibers and non-ferrous material, offering smooth and quiet braking performance, suitable for everyday riding or commuting. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of motorcycle riders in the market.</p></p>
