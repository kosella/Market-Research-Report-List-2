<p><p>High-Volume Degaussers are used to erase data from magnetic storage devices in bulk. There are three primary market types: Coil Degaussers, which use a powerful electromagnetic coil to create a fluctuating magnetic field; Capacitive Discharge Degaussers, which discharge energy stored in capacitors to generate a magnetic field; and Permanent Magnet Degaussers, which use strong permanent magnets to erase data. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications based on their specific capabilities.</p></p>
