<p><p>Adhesion testers market includes various types of machines used for testing the adhesion strength of materials. Tensile testing machines measure the force required to pull materials apart. Friction testing machines measure the force needed to overcome the resistance between two surfaces. Peel testing machines measure the strength required to peel apart two bonded materials. Multifunctional testing machines combine various testing methods for comprehensive evaluation. Other types of adhesion testers include specialized machines for specific testing needs.</p></p>
