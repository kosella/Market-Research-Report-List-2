<p><p>Safe radar sensors are categorized into two main types: Imaging radar sensors and non-imaging radar sensors. Imaging radar sensors provide high-resolution images and detailed information about the surroundings, making them suitable for applications like autonomous driving and surveillance systems. On the other hand, non-imaging radar sensors focus on detecting the presence and movement of objects without generating detailed images. They are commonly used in applications such as collision avoidance systems and industrial automation. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing safety and efficiency across various industries.</p></p>
