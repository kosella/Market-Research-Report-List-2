<p><p>Amblyopia glasses market types include Deprivation Amblyopia, which occurs when there is a blockage or obstruction in the vision of one eye; Refractive Amblyopia, which is caused by a significant difference in the refractive error between the two eyes; Strabismic Amblyopia, which results from misalignment of the eyes leading to poor vision development in one eye.
