<p><p>Metal use jigsaw blades come in two main types - T-Shank and U-Shank. T-Shank blades have a T-shaped design that allows for quick and easy blade changes without the need for additional tools. They are widely used in modern jigsaws due to their convenience and efficiency. On the other hand, U-Shank blades have a U-shaped design and are compatible with older models of jigsaws. Both types of blades come in various sizes and designs to cater to different cutting needs in the market.</p></p>
