<p><p>The car radial tire market can be segmented into three main types: all-steel, semi-steel, and other. All-steel tires are made entirely of steel belts and cords, providing excellent strength and durability. Semi-steel tires combine steel belts with other materials such as fabric or polyester for a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness. The "other" market includes specialty tires made with different combinations of materials to cater to specific needs, such as off-road or high-speed performance. Each type offers unique advantages and is targeted towards different consumers.</p></p>
