<p><strong>The On-board Diagnostic (OBD) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threshold Monitoring</li><li>Comprehensive Component Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The On-board Diagnostic (OBD) System market comprises two main types - Threshold Monitoring and Comprehensive Component Monitoring. Threshold Monitoring systems monitor specific thresholds for various parameters such as engine performance, emissions, and fuel efficiency. On the other hand, Comprehensive Component Monitoring systems continuously monitor and diagnose individual components within the vehicle, providing detailed insights into the health and performance of each part. Both types of OBD systems play a crucial role in ensuring vehicle safety, performance, and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
