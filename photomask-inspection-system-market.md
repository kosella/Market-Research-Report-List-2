<p><strong>The Photomask Inspection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photomask Inspection System market includes two main types: Die to Die (DD) Method and Die to Database (DB) Method. Die to Die method compares one die to another whereas Die to Database method compares a die to a computer-generated reference database. DD method is primarily used for defect detection on small changes in pattern structures while DB method is useful for overlay and critical dimension measurements. Both methods are essential in ensuring the quality and accuracy of photomasks used in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
