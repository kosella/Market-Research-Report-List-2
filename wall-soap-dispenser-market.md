<p><p>Wall soap dispensers come in two main types: manual and automatic. Manual dispensers require the user to physically push a button or pump to release the soap, which is a more traditional and cost-effective option. On the other hand, automatic dispensers use sensors to detect when hands are near and dispense the soap automatically, offering a more convenient and hygienic solution. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the market for wall-mounted soap dispensers.</p></p>
