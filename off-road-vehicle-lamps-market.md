<p><p>Off-Road Vehicle Lamps can be broadly categorized into three types - Halogen, HID (High-Intensity Discharge) and LED (Light Emitting Diode). Halogen lamps are known for their affordability and ease of replacement, but have lower energy efficiency. HID lamps produce bright light and have longer lifespan, but can be more expensive. LED lamps are the most energy-efficient option with the longest lifespan, providing bright light and durability. Each type offers unique advantages and caters to different preferences and needs in the Off-Road Vehicle Lamps market.</p></p>
