<p><ul><li>Helical Gear Drive</li><li>Bevel Gear Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Helical Gear Drive and Bevel Gear Drive are two main types of gearboxes in the market. Helical Gear Drive is known for its efficiency and smooth operation due to the helical teeth that are cut at an angle to the gear axis, providing quieter operation and higher torque capacity. On the other hand, Bevel Gear Drive is used for applications where power transmission needs to change directions, such as in right angle drives or intersecting shafts. Both types have unique features that cater to different industrial and mechanical needs.</p></p>
