<p><strong>The Stationary Barcode Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Scanner</li><li>Linear Imager</li><li>2D Imager Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary barcode scanners are available in three main types: Laser Scanner, Linear Imager, and 2D Imager Scanner. Laser scanners use a beam of light to read barcodes, offering high-speed scanning and long-range capabilities. Linear imagers use LED lights to capture barcode images, making them suitable for reading damaged or poorly printed barcodes. 2D imagers can read both 1D and 2D barcodes, offering versatility and advanced data capture capabilities. Each type has its own strengths and is suited for different applications in the stationary barcode scanner market.</p></p>
