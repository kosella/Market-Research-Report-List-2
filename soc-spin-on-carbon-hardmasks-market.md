<p><p>SOC (Spin on Carbon) Hardmasks are used in semiconductor manufacturing to transfer patterns onto silicon wafers. There are two main types of SOC Hardmasks: Hot-Temperature Spin on Carbon Hardmasks and Normal Spin on Carbon Hardmasks. Hot-Temperature Spin on Carbon Hardmasks are designed to withstand high processing temperatures, making them suitable for advanced semiconductor fabrication processes. Normal Spin on Carbon Hardmasks, on the other hand, are used in standard semiconductor manufacturing processes that do not require high-temperature resistance. Each type of hardmask serves a specific purpose in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
