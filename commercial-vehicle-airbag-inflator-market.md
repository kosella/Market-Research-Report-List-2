<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Airbag Inflator Market is segmented into three types- Pyrotechnic Inflator, Stored Gas Inflator, and Hybrid Inflator. Pyrotechnic inflators use a chemical reaction to rapidly generate gas to inflate the airbag. Stored Gas inflators store compressed gas which is released to inflate the airbag when triggered. Hybrid inflators combine both pyrotechnic and stored gas technologies for efficient airbag deployment. Each type has its unique advantages and applications in the commercial vehicle industry.</p></p>
