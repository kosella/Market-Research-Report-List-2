<p><strong>The Automotive Camera Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS)</li><li>Charge-Coupled Device (CCD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive camera sensor market includes two main types: Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS) and Charge-Coupled Device (CCD). CMOS sensors are known for their low power consumption, higher integration capabilities, and faster processing speeds, making them popular in modern automotive camera systems. On the other hand, CCD sensors offer superior image quality, especially in low light conditions, but they consume more power and are bulkier compared to CMOS sensors. Both types play a crucial role in enabling advanced driver assistance systems and enhancing overall vehicle safety.</p></p>
