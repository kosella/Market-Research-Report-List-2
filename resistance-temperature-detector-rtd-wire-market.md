<p><p>Resistance Temperature Detector (RTD) Wire market is divided into two main types: Thin-film RTD and Wire-wound RTD. Thin-film RTDs are made by depositing a thin film of platinum on a ceramic substrate, which provides fast response time and accurate temperature measurements. Wire-wound RTDs consist of a coil of platinum wire wrapped around a ceramic core, offering high accuracy and stability over a wide temperature range. Both types are commonly used in industrial applications where precise temperature measurement is required.</p></p>
