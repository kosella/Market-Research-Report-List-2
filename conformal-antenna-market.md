<p><p>Conformal antennas come in different shapes and types to suit various applications. Cylindrical antennas are commonly used in military and aerospace industries due to their compact size and omnidirectional radiation patterns. Round antennas are preferred for their symmetrical radiation characteristics and are commonly used in satellite communication systems. Spherical antennas provide a 360-degree coverage area and are used for applications requiring full spherical coverage. Straight antennas are simple and cost-effective, commonly used in consumer electronics and wireless communication devices.</p></p>
