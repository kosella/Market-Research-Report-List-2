<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fluorescence Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Epifluorescence Microscopy</li><li>Inverted Fluorescence Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorescence microscopes are specialized instruments used for imaging fluorescent samples. Epifluorescence microscopy involves illuminating the sample from above and capturing the emitted light through the same objective lens. Inverted fluorescence microscopes, on the other hand, illuminate the sample from below and capture the emitted light through the objective lens above the sample. Both types of microscopes are widely used in biological research, medical diagnostics, and materials science for studying fluorescence in various samples.</p></p>
