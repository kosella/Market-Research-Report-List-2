<p><ul><li>Ceramic Core Multi-layering Chip Inductor</li><li>Magnetic Core Multi-layering Chip Inductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi-layering chip inductors are classified into two types based on their core material. Ceramic core multi-layering chip inductors are made with a ceramic core that provides high reliability and stability. On the other hand, magnetic core multi-layering chip inductors use a magnetic core material to achieve higher inductance values. Both types of chip inductors are widely used in various electronic applications such as smartphones, computers, and automotive electronics due to their compact size and excellent performance characteristics.</p></p>
