<p><p>GPS Sonobuoy market can be categorized into three types: Active Sonobuoy, Passive Sonobuoy, and Special Purpose Sonobuoy. Active Sonobuoys transmit acoustic signals to detect underwater targets, while Passive Sonobuoys listen for sound waves emitted by the target. Special Purpose Sonobuoys are designed for specific missions such as anti-submarine warfare or oceanographic research. Each type of sonobuoy plays a unique role in enhancing underwater surveillance and tracking capabilities, catering to diverse needs in defense, security, and scientific applications.</p></p>
