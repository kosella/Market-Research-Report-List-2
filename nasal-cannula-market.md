<p><p>Nasal cannulas are commonly used devices in healthcare settings to deliver oxygen to patients with respiratory problems. There are two main types of nasal cannulas available in the market: low-flow nasal cannulas and high-flow nasal cannulas. Low-flow nasal cannulas deliver a lower flow rate of oxygen and are suitable for patients with mild to moderate respiratory issues. High-flow nasal cannulas, on the other hand, deliver oxygen at a higher flow rate and are typically used for patients with severe respiratory conditions or those requiring higher oxygen levels.</p></p>
