<p><ul><li>Rosin Ester Series</li><li>Terpene Phenolic Resin Series</li><li>Polymeric Rosin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tackifier resin dispersions are classified into three main types: Rosin Ester Series, Terpene Phenolic Resin Series, and Polymeric Rosin. Rosin Ester Series is derived from rosin and offers excellent tackifying properties. Terpene Phenolic Resin Series combines terpene and phenolic components for enhanced adhesion. Polymeric Rosin is a synthetic resin that provides flexibility and improved performance. Each type offers unique characteristics that cater to different applications in industries such as adhesives, coatings, and sealants.</p></p>
