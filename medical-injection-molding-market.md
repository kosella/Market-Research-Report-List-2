<p><strong>The Medical Injection Molding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Runner</li><li>Hot Runner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical injection molding market includes two main types: Cold Runner and Hot Runner systems. Cold Runner system is cost-effective, as it uses a simple design with a solidified channel for the plastic material to flow through, leading to easier maintenance and lower production costs. On the other hand, Hot Runner system is more efficient and suitable for high-volume production, as it uses an insulated runner system to keep the plastic material in a molten state, resulting in reduced material waste and shorter cycle times.</p></p>
