<p><p>Solar controllers are devices used to regulate the voltage and current generated by solar panels to ensure efficient charging of batteries. The three main types of solar controllers are Ordinary Solar Controller, PWM Solar Controller, and MPPT Solar Controller. Ordinary controllers are the basic form of solar controllers, PWM controllers are more efficient at regulating charging currents, and MPPT controllers are the most advanced and efficient in converting solar energy into electric power. Each type serves different purposes based on the specific requirements of the solar system.</p></p>
