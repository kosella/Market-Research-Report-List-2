<p><p>Molecular pumps are essential in semiconductor devices for maintaining high vacuum levels during manufacturing processes. There are three main types of molecular pumps used in the industry - Magnetic Levitation Molecular Pump, Oil Lubricated Molecular Pump, and Grease Lubricated Molecular Pump.</p><p>- Magnetic Levitation Molecular Pumps use magnetic levitation to eliminate contact between moving parts, reducing friction and wear.</p><p>- Oil Lubricated Molecular Pumps utilize oil to lubricate moving parts and ensure efficient operation.</p><p>- Grease Lubricated Molecular Pumps employ grease for lubrication, offering a cost-effective solution for vacuum systems.</p></p>
