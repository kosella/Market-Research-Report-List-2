<p><p>Roller blinds are available in two main market types: manual roller blinds and motorised roller blinds. Manual roller blinds are operated by pulling a chain or cord to raise or lower the blinds. They are cost-effective and easy to use but require physical effort to operate. On the other hand, motorised roller blinds are controlled by a motor mechanism, allowing for remote operation via a switch or smartphone app. While motorised roller blinds are more expensive, they offer convenience and a modern touch to any space.</p></p>
