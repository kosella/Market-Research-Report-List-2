Active Detection Systems use sensors to actively monitor for smoke or fire in real-time, often triggering alarms or alerts. Passive Detection Systems, on the other hand, rely on cameras and image processing technology to passively detect smoke or fire based on visual cues. Both systems play a crucial role in enhancing fire safety and early fire detection in various settings like commercial buildings, residential complexes, and industrial facilities.</p></p>
