They are available in various types such as Expanded Polystyrene (EPS), Extruded Polystyrene (XPS), and Polyurethane (PUR) panels. EPS panels are lightweight and cost-effective, XPS panels offer high thermal resistance, and PUR panels provide superior insulation properties. The choice of SIP type depends on the specific needs of the construction project.</p></p>
