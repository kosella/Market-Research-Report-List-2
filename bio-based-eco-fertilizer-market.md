<p><p>Bio-based (Eco) fertilizers are classified into different types based on their nutrient-producing abilities. Nitrogen-fixing fertilizers convert atmospheric nitrogen into a form plants can use. Phosphate-solubilizing fertilizers release phosphorus for plant uptake. Potash-mobilizing fertilizers aid in the absorption of potassium by plants. Other types of bio-based fertilizers include those that improve soil structure, enhance nutrient uptake, and stimulate plant growth. These eco-friendly fertilizers help maintain soil health and reduce the reliance on synthetic chemicals.</p></p>
