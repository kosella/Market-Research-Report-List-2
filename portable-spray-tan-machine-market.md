<p><p>Portable spray tan machines come in two main types: Low Volume Low Pressure (LVLP) and High Volume Low Pressure (HVLP). LVLP machines are known for their efficiency in spray tan application, using less product and creating a more precise finish. On the other hand, HVLP machines are known for their high spray volume, covering a larger surface area quickly. Both types cater to different preferences and needs, offering customers options based on their desired results and spray tan application experience.</p></p>
