<p><strong>The Enhanced PoE Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>802.3af PoE</li><li>802.3at PoE +</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enhanced PoE switches come in two main types: 802.3af PoE and 802.3at PoE+. The 802.3af PoE standard provides up to 15.4 watts of power per port, while the 802.3at PoE+ standard offers up to 30 watts per port. The main difference between the two is the amount of power they can deliver, with PoE+ being able to support higher power devices such as PTZ cameras, VoIP phones, and wireless access points. Both standards provide power and data over a single Ethernet cable, reducing the need for additional power sources.</p></p>
