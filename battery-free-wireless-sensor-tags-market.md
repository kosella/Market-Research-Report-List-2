<p><p>Active tags have their own power source and are capable of transmitting data to a reader without the need of external energy. Passive tags rely on the reader to provide the necessary power for data transmission. Semi-active tags have a power source to run certain functions, but still rely on the reader for communication. Each type of tag has its own strengths and weaknesses, making them suitable for different applications based on factors such as range, cost, and power efficiency.</p></p>
