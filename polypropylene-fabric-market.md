<p><strong>The Polypropylene Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homopolymer Polypropylene</li><li>Copolymer Polypropylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polypropylene fabric market is mainly divided into two types - homopolymer polypropylene and copolymer polypropylene. Homopolymer polypropylene is a type of polypropylene that is made from a single type of monomer, resulting in a high level of crystallinity and stiffness. On the other hand, copolymer polypropylene is made from a mixture of different monomers, which provides enhanced flexibility and impact resistance. Both types of polypropylene have their own unique properties and applications in various industries such as packaging, automotive, and textiles.</p></p>
