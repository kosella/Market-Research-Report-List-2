<p><p>High Speed Horizontal Cartoners come in two main types: Semi Automatic and Fully Automatic. Semi Automatic cartoners require some manual intervention from operators, such as loading products into the machine and closing cartons. Fully Automatic cartoners operate with minimal human involvement, automating the entire cartoning process from start to finish. Both types offer increased efficiency and speed compared to manual cartoning processes, making them popular choices for industries requiring high volume cartoning operations.</p></p>
