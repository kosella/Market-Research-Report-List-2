<p><p>The Specialty Adhesive Tapes Market can be classified into three main types based on the adhesive composition used: Solvent-based, Hot-Melt based, and Water-based. Solvent-based tapes use chemical solvents to create a strong bond, Hot-Melt based tapes are adhesive when heated and solidify when cooled, and Water-based tapes use water as a carrier for the adhesive. Each type offers unique benefits and applications, catering to specific industry needs and requirements. These varieties cater to a wide range of industries, from automotive to packaging.</p></p>
