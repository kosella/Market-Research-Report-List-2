<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor market includes two main types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors utilize a Hall effect sensor to measure wheel speed, providing accurate readings for various applications. On the other hand, Magnetic Electric Type sensors use magnetic fields to detect wheel speed changes, offering a reliable and cost-effective solution. Both types play a crucial role in improving the performance and safety of new energy vehicles by ensuring precise control of speed and acceleration.</p></p>
