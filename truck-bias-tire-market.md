<p><ul><li>Ordinary Bias Tires</li><li>Bias Belted Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Truck bias tire market consists of ordinary bias tires and bias belted tires. Ordinary bias tires have multiple layers of ply cords overlapping each other at angles, providing stability and durability. These tires are commonly used in heavy-duty trucks for long distance travel. On the other hand, bias belted tires have an additional belt under the tread for increased strength and improved tread wear. These tires are preferred for their improved handling and stability, making them suitable for various road conditions.</p></p>
