<p><p>Laminated glass is categorized into different types based on the interlayer material used. PVB (Polyvinyl Butyral) is the most common interlayer material, providing good adhesion and sound insulation. EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) is known for its high impact resistance and UV protection properties. SGP (SentryGlas Plus) is a premium interlayer material offering superior strength and durability. Other types of interlayers include ionoplast and thermoplastic materials, each with their unique characteristics to cater to specific application needs in the laminated glass market.</p></p>
