<p><strong>The Photomask Blank Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Reflectance Photomask Blank</li><li>Medium Reflectance Photomask Blank</li><li>High Reflectivity Photomask Blank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photomask blanks are classified into three types based on their reflectance levels: low reflectance, medium reflectance, and high reflectivity. Low reflectance photomask blanks are designed for deep ultraviolet lithography processes, offering minimal light reflection for high-precision imaging. Medium reflectance photomask blanks provide a balance between reflectance and absorption, suitable for a wide range of lithography applications. High reflectivity photomask blanks are ideal for electron beam lithography, maximizing light reflection to achieve detailed patterns with extreme precision.</p></p>
