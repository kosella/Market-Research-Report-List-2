<p><p>GFCI outlets, or Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter outlets, are specially designed electrical outlets that are equipped with sensors to detect any imbalance in the current flow. If the sensors detect even a small leakage of current, the GFCI outlet automatically shuts off the power to prevent electric shock. These outlets are commonly installed in areas where water and electricity are in close proximity, such as bathrooms, kitchens, and outdoor spaces.</p><p>The GFCI outlets market is witnessing significant growth, with increasing awareness about electrical safety standards and regulations driving demand for these outlets.
