<p><strong>The Harvester Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Agriculture Tires</li><li>Bias (Crossply) Agriculture Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Harvester tires are classified into two main types: radial agriculture tires and bias (crossply) agriculture tires. Radial agriculture tires are designed with a radial ply structure which offers better traction and lower soil compaction. Bias (crossply) agriculture tires, on the other hand, have a diagonal ply structure that provides high flotation and resistance to damage. Both types of tires have their own advantages and are utilized based on specific requirements and conditions in the agricultural industry.</p></p>
