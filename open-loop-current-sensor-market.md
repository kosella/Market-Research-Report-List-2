<p><ul><li>Isolated</li><li>Non-Isolated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Open loop current sensors are available in two main types: isolated and non-isolated. Isolated current sensors provide electrical isolation between the current-carrying conductor and the output circuit, making them suitable for high-voltage applications where safety and protection are critical. Non-isolated sensors do not have this isolation, but they are more cost-effective and simpler to implement. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications based on the specific requirements of the system.</p></p>
