<p><p>The 5G electromagnetic wave shield film market includes two main types: metal-mesh film and coating film. Metal-mesh film consists of a thin layer of metal deposited on a substrate, providing effective shielding against electromagnetic waves. On the other hand, coating film involves applying a special coating on surfaces to block the penetration of electromagnetic radiation. Both types of films are widely used in electronics and telecommunications industries to protect devices and equipment from interference and potential health risks associated with exposure to electromagnetic waves.</p></p>
