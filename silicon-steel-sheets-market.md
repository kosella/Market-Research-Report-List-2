Oriented electrical steel is used in applications where a strong magnetic field is needed, such as transformers and electric motors. Non-grain oriented electrical steel is more commonly used in rotating machinery and small transformers. Both types of silicon steel sheets play a crucial role in ensuring efficient energy transfer and distribution in various electrical devices.</p></p>
