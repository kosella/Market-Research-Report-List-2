<p><strong>The Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Biosensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Delay-line type</li><li>Resonators Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Biosensors can be categorized into two main types: Delay-line type and Resonators Type. </p><p>Delay-line type biosensors utilize the delay in the signal caused by the binding of molecules to the surface of the sensor to detect biomolecular interactions. </p><p>Resonators type biosensors, on the other hand, rely on changes in the resonant frequency of the acoustic wave due to the presence of biomolecules on the sensor surface. </p><p>Both types of SAW biosensors are widely used in applications such as healthcare, environmental monitoring, and food safety.</p></p>
