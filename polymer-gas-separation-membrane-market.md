<p><p>The Polymer Gas Separation Membrane Market is segmented into three main types: Hollow Fiber, Spiral Wound, and Others. </p><p>Hollow Fiber membranes consist of a bundle of porous tubes that allow gas molecules to pass through while restricting undesired molecules. Spiral Wound membranes are constructed by wrapping a flat membrane layer around a permeate tube, offering high efficiency in gas separation. The "Others" category includes different types of polymer membranes with unique structures and functionalities for specific gas separation applications. Each type offers distinct advantages and is used in various industries for gas separation processes.</p></p>
