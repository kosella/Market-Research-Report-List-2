<p><p>Commercial Ethernet Switches are divided into three main types: Fixed Managed, Fixed Unmanaged, and Modular Switches. Fixed Managed switches offer advanced features like VLAN support and Quality of Service settings, ideal for larger networks. Fixed Unmanaged switches are simple plug-and-play devices suitable for smaller networks with basic networking needs. Modular switches allow for customization by adding different modules for various functionalities, making them versatile for businesses with evolving networking requirements. Each type caters to different network sizes and complexity levels.</p></p>
