<p><p>EMI common mode inductors play a crucial role in filtering electromagnetic interference in electronic circuits. There are three main types of EMI common mode inductors available in the market: winding chip type, multilayer chip, and through hole type. Winding chip type inductors offer compact designs suitable for high-density PCB layouts. Multilayer chip inductors provide high inductance values in a small footprint. Through hole type inductors are more traditional and offer easy assembly and robust mechanical support. Each type caters to different application requirements in the EMI filtering industry.</p></p>
