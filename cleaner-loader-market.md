<p><p>Cleaner-Loader machines are available in different market types, including self-propelled, trailed, and semi-mounted options. Self-propelled Cleaner-Loaders are equipped with their own engine and are ideal for larger cleaning and loading tasks. Trailed Cleaner-Loaders are pulled by a tractor and are suitable for medium-sized operations. Semi-mounted Cleaner-Loaders are attached to the tractor by a 3-point linkage, offering flexibility in terms of maneuverability and efficiency. Each type caters to different needs and requirements in the agricultural and industrial sectors.</p></p>
