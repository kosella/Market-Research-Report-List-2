<p><p>Stainless steel orthodontic wires come in two main types: multi-strand and single strand. Multi-strand stainless steel wire is made up of several small strands braided together, offering flexibility and resistance to bending. On the other hand, single strand stainless steel wire is a solid, single piece, providing strength and durability. Both types are commonly used in orthodontic treatments to move and align teeth, but the choice between them depends on the specific requirements of each patient's treatment.</p></p>
