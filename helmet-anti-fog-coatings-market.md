<p><ul><li>Hydrophilic Coating</li><li>Nanocoating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Helmet Anti-Fog Coatings Market includes two main types of coatings - Hydrophilic Coating and Nanocoating. Hydrophilic coatings work by absorbing water vapor, which prevents the formation of fog on the helmet visor. On the other hand, nanocoatings use nanoparticles to create a thin, invisible layer on the helmet visor that repels water molecules, preventing fogging. Both types of coatings offer effective fog prevention solutions for helmet wearers in various activities such as motorcycling, skiing, and biking.</p></p>
