<p><strong>The Dishwashing Detergents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Saponification</li><li>Non-saponification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dishwashing detergents can be classified into two main types based on their chemical composition: saponification and non-saponification detergents. Saponification detergents contain fatty acids that react with alkalis to form soap, effectively breaking down grease and grime. Non-saponification detergents, on the other hand, rely on synthetic surfactants to emulsify and remove dirt from dishes. Both types are widely used in the market, catering to different consumer preferences and cleaning needs.</p></p>
