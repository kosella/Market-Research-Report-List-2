<p><ul><li>OCT Camera</li><li>Fundus Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra Wide Field Retinal Imaging Device market includes two main types of devices: OCT cameras and Fundus cameras. OCT cameras use light waves to capture detailed cross-sectional images of the retina, allowing for early detection and monitoring of eye conditions. Fundus cameras, on the other hand, provide high-resolution images of the back of the eye, enabling healthcare professionals to assess the health of the retina and optic nerve. Both types of devices play a crucial role in diagnosing and managing various eye diseases.</p></p>
