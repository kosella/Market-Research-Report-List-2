<p><strong>The Mill Liner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal Mill Liner</li><li>Rubber Mill Liner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal Mill Liners are typically made of steel and are used in heavy-duty grinding applications where high impact and abrasion resistance is required. They are known for their durability and longevity, making them ideal for mining and mineral processing industries. On the other hand, Rubber Mill Liners are made of rubber and are preferred for their noise reduction and impact absorption properties. They are commonly used in applications where a softer lining material is needed to protect the mill shell and reduce wear and tear on the equipment.</p></p>
