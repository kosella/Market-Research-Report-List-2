<p><p>Cloud RAN and Centralized RAN are two types of Mobile Fronthaul markets that are becoming increasingly popular. Cloud RAN refers to a network architecture where baseband processing is centralized in a data center, allowing for more efficient resource allocation and lower operating costs. Centralized RAN, on the other hand, involves moving the baseband units to a central location, leading to improved coordination between cells. Both approaches offer benefits such as enhanced network performance, scalability, and flexibility for mobile operators looking to optimize their networks.</p></p>
