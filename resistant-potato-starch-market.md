<p><p>Resistant potato starch is classified into two main types in the market: RS2 and RS3. RS2 refers to physically modified resistant starch, which retains its structure and resistance to digestion. On the other hand, RS3 is chemically modified resistant starch, which undergoes a process to increase its resistance to digestion. Both types of resistant potato starch offer unique properties and benefits, catering to different preferences and applications in the food industry.</p></p>
