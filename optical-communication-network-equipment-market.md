<p><ul><li>Access Network</li><li>Metropolitan Area Network</li><li>Trunk Network</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Communication Network Equipment Market can be categorized into three main types: Access Network, Metropolitan Area Network, and Trunk Network. </p><p>Access Network refers to the equipment used to connect end-users to the network, such as fiber optic cables, switches, and routers. </p><p>Metropolitan Area Network includes equipment that connects multiple LANs within a city or metropolitan area, enabling fast and efficient communication. </p><p>Trunk Network involves equipment for connecting large volumes of data between cities or long distances, typically using high-capacity fiber optic cables and routers.</p></p>
