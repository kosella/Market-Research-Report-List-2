<p><p>Vacuum pouches are available in various types such as PE, PET, PP, NY, and AL. PE (polyethylene) pouches are commonly used for food packaging due to their flexibility and durability. PET (polyethylene terephthalate) pouches provide excellent clarity and barrier properties. PP (polypropylene) pouches are known for their high heat resistance and stiffness. NY (nylon) pouches offer exceptional puncture resistance and oxygen barrier properties. AL (aluminum) pouches are used for sensitive products that require protection from light and moisture. Each type caters to specific packaging needs in the market.</p></p>
