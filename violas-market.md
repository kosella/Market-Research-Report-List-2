<p><p>Violas are available in two main market types: Acoustic Violas and Electric Violas. Acoustic Violas produce sound through the vibration of the instrument's strings, creating a warm and natural tone. They are commonly used in classical and orchestral settings. On the other hand, Electric Violas have pickups that convert string vibrations into electrical signals, allowing them to be amplified. They are popular in modern music genres and offer a wide range of sounds through effects and electronic manipulation. Both types cater to different musical preferences and styles.</p></p>
