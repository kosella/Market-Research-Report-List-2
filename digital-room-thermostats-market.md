<p><ul><li>Millivolt Thermostats</li><li>24 Volt Thermostats</li><li>Line Voltage Thermostats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital room thermostats are available in different types based on the input voltage they operate on. Millivolt thermostats are designed for systems that use millivolt control, such as some gas fireplaces. 24 volt thermostats are commonly used for low voltage systems like central heating and cooling. Line voltage thermostats are used in systems that run on standard household voltage, such as baseboard heaters. Each type of thermostat caters to specific voltage requirements, ensuring compatibility with various heating and cooling systems.</p></p>
